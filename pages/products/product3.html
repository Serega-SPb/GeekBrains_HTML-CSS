Подробное описание товара

The domestic high quality figure maker Gecco has started a figure project of the hit action RPG series «DARK SOULS».
The latest release is a 1/6 scale polystone statue of «Siegmeyer of Catarina», extremely popular character with
a chubby armor and a helmet that looks like an onion!
He makes a few appearances as the story progresses and is usually worrying over something or falling asleep.
In the tough world of «Dark Souls»., many players surely found a moment of peace when looking at him.
This lovable character has been turned into a statue under the complete supervision of the FromSoftware design team.
Beside his characteristic armor, the impressive texture of the sculpture that lets you feels his long
fighting history and the paintwork are recreated with exceptional Gecco quality! Due to its shape it has
a massive volume that makes its look bigger than 1/6 scale. The decorations on the chest and back,
the chain mail sagging from the openings of the armor, the base that looks like a piece of the «Firelink Shrine»
and even the character’s face slightly visible from the slit on his helmet have all been finely sculpted by Gecco,
creating a real masterpiece.
The sculpture was created by Shinya Akao (HEADLONG), who is also a big fan of «DARK SOULS». Looking at the statue
you can perceive his love for the character. The colored sample was painted by Katsuhige Akeyama (-accent-).
Be sure to check out the amazingly realistic paintwork!
