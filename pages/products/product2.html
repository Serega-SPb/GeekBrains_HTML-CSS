From "4 Goddesses Online CYBER DIMENSION NEPTUNE" comes a figure of the Holy Knight Neptune.
The goddess guarding Planeptune, the powerful protagonist and face of the series,
Neptune is made into a figure that captures her cheerful, optimistic nature and excessively hyper personality.
Knight armor’s detail and moving hair’s finer points are accurately recreated while the softness of
the outfit’s fabric is expressed through the countless folds and shade of the paint color for a
finished product that really lets you feel Neptune’s mysterious charms.
